About School

St' Xavius High School also know as SXHS promises you to give you'r child a Bright future ahead .

Vision

It is the vision of SPHS to lay the foundation of confidence,creativity and capability in it’s students, enabling them to blossom and scale new heights in their chosen fields in an ever emerging dynamic world.

Mission

It is the mission of SPHS to let every child blossom into a life-long learner in it’s safe and secure nurturing environment through Belief, Initiative, Courage and Passion;engaging learners purposefully in study, skill, sport and service for Human excellence.

Philosophy

The endeavor is to LEAD students towards Human Excellence through Academic Excellence and 21stcentury skills such as Communicative, Creative, Collaborative, Critical thinking and Computational skills that build their confidence to take up knowledge construction while being rooted to their own values,culture with a mindset to be both modern and democratic.

Our STRENGTHS:

We correlate education with problems of life and prepare for Life. We democratize education through Learner Autonomy. We cope with the problems arising out of the modern scientific and technical advancement through value-based learning situations. We take education from knowledge dissemination to student-centred knowledge construction through Intelligent Conversations.

Whole Brain Classrooms

SXHS provides an array of Learning activities that nurture thinking preferences of 8 dimensions. SXHS challenges the students’ Multiple Intelligences’ Quotient through an array of learning activities such as Vocabulary Building Activities, Creative Writing Tasks, Worksheets, Assessment of speaking and listening skills, Quizzes, Symposia, Group Discussions, Research Work, Data Interpretation, Problem Solving Tasks, Case Studies, Investigative Projects, Model making, Presentations, Seminars, Model UN Sessions , Life Skills Tasks, Role plays, Experiments, Lab Activities, Field Trips, Inter-disciplinary/Uni-disciplinary Projects etc.Assessments are standardized as per Bloom’s Taxonomy to raise the students’ Emotional, Social and Competitive Quotients apart from their Intelligence Quotient.

LEARNING ETHOS at SPHS

The starting point of learning at St.Xavius is a positive ethos and a culture of Trust and Be Entrusted. Our Edu- Ethos enhances the Dignity of a Human-being through Activity-based Learning, increases his/her self-respect and Universal Brotherhood in its true sense; building the sheet of anchor for every learner at St.Xavius High School.The 5 –step Experiential approach (AIM – ACTION- ANALYSIS –APPLICATION-ASSESSMENT ) designed by XSEED is practiced in SXHS classrooms to build thinking skills, problem-solving skills, nurture higher grade creative expression and boost confidence levels paving the way for learner autonomy. SXHS is rated one of the best XSEED Curriculum schools in the country.
